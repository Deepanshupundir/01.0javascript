Virat kohli

On May 30, 1998, a young Virat Kohli and his father, Prem Kohli, entered the West Delhi Cricket Academy (WDCA), marking the beginning of a remarkable journey. Virats deep desire to become a cricketer was evident even at the age of nine, and his father s support was unwavering. Raj Kumar Sharma, the academys coach, was struck by Virat s intense focus and determination.


MS Dhoni

Early life. Dhoni was born on 7 July 1981 in Ranchi, Bihar (now in Jharkhand) in a Hindu Rajput family to Pan Singh and Devaki Devi. His parents hailed from Lwali village in Uttar Pradesh (now Uttarakhand) and he was the youngest of three children. His family spells the surname as "Dhauni".


Rohit Sharma

Sharma was born on April 30, 1987, in Bansod, Nagpur, in the state of Maharashtra, India. He first joined a cricket camp in 1999, when coach Dinesh Lad, a friend of his uncle's, noticed his talent. Lad used to scout for talent in those days and was very impressed with the way Sharma bowled.



Yuvraj Singh

Early years and personal life. Yuvraj Singh was born in a Punjabi Jat Sikh family to Yograj Singh, a former India cricketer, and Shabnam Singh. Tennis and roller skating were Yuvraj's favourite sports during his childhood and he was quite good at both. He had also won the National Under-14 Roller Skating Championship
